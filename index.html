Precision That Powers Production Lines Worldwide

Engineering-driven, quality-obsessed, and built on a decade of real mold-making experience.

Gege Mould operates a 3,000-square-meter advanced manufacturing facility in the Huangyan District of Taizhou — a region known globally as one of the most concentrated mold-making industrial zones in the world. From this base, we deliver injection molds and molding services to customers across more than 30 countries. As an OEM automotive parts supplier and custom plastic parts manufacturer, we serve the full spectrum of the automotive supply chain — from OEM programs to Tier 1 and Tier 2 automotive supplier partnerships.

Our focus is straightforward: combine rigorous engineering discipline with practical shop-floor experience to build molds that perform reliably in production. Every tool we ship has been designed with full DFM analysis, machined on advanced CNC and EDM equipment, trialed in-house with production-grade materials, and inspected against your dimensional specifications before crating. Whether you're submitting an automotive parts manufacturer RFQ for a complete bumper tooling program or sourcing a tooling and mold supplier for precision connector molds, our process delivers the same level of engineering rigor.

We serve automotive OEMs and Tier 1/2 suppliers across passenger vehicle, heavy-duty truck, and commercial vehicle segments — each with different requirements, but all with the same expectation: a mold that works, on time, to spec. As an injection molded parts manufacturer and automotive component manufacturer, that is precisely what we deliver.
