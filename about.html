Why Gege Mould Stands Apart

  • 10+ years of specialized mold engineering — deeply experienced in automotive-grade tooling for bumper, door panel, lamp, and functional part molds trusted by Mercedes-Benz, Volvo, Scania, DAF, MAN, IVECO, and Renault.
  • 3,000 m² manufacturing facility located in Taizhou's Huangyan District — the heart of China's mold-making industry — with advanced CNC, EDM, and quality inspection equipment under one roof.

Engineering Precision Since Day One

Gege Mould was founded with a clear mission: to provide world-class plastic injection mold engineering and manufacturing from the heart of China's mold-making capital in Taizhou, Zhejiang Province.

With more than ten years of hands-on experience in the mold industry, we have grown into a trusted partner for automotive OEMs and industrial manufacturers across Europe, North America, and Asia. Our 3,000-square-meter facility houses advanced CNC machining centers, EDM equipment, and a dedicated quality inspection department — all organized around efficient, lean production workflows.

What sets Gege Mould apart is our commitment to the entire mold lifecycle. We don't just cut steel — we collaborate with your engineering team from the DFM (Design for Manufacturing) stage through to production ramp-up, ensuring every tool we deliver performs exactly as specified.

Gege Mould is a brand of Taizhou Jingling Moulding Technology Co., Ltd., and operates as part of the same manufacturing group as Automotive Mould Factory. Together we share production facilities, engineering resources, and a combined track record of successful mold programs with leading international brands.

Our Team

The engineers and technicians behind every mold we deliver — combining decades of mold-making experience with modern manufacturing discipline.

Gege Mould is powered by a tight-knit team of mold design engineers, CNC programmers, toolmakers, and quality inspectors — many of whom have been with the company for over a decade. Our team's hands-on experience spans the full mold-making process: from initial DFM analysis and CAD/CAM programming through precision machining, assembly, trial, and final inspection.

When you work with Gege Mould, your first conversation is with an engineer, not a sales representative. We believe that real mold-making expertise starts on the shop floor — and that's where our team lives.

Chairman & Founder

Jake Knox

With deep roots in Taizhou's mold-making industry — the most concentrated mold manufacturing zone in the world — our chairman has built Gege Mould from the ground up as a precision-driven automotive injection mold manufacturer. Under this leadership, the company has grown from a local tooling workshop into a 3,000-square-meter advanced facility serving global automotive OEM, Tier 1, and Tier 2 customers across more than 30 countries.

A firm believer that "a mold is not just a tool — it is a promise of quality to the customer", the chairman personally reviews every major tool program before it leaves the facility. This hands-on, engineering-first philosophy permeates every level of the organization — from the CAD station to the CMM lab to the tryout press.

Today, Gege Mould operates as part of Taizhou Jingling Moulding Technology Co., Ltd. and shares facilities, engineering resources, and a combined track record with Automotive Mould Factory — delivering precision injection molds.
